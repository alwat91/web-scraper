Honest and Positive Experienceby Patrick J.

Moving is stressful at best, but Best Moving made it very bearable and really much less stressful. The initial estimate given to us by Larry was exactly what we were charged. The movers in the Houston area worked non-stop and packed up our entire house. We had them do all of the packing as well, so it was a long day. Their operations department was in constant communication about when our things would arrive in Chicago, and the same team that loaded us in Texas were the same ones who unloaded everything in Chicago. All the furniture was put together and they put every box exactly where it belonged, which made my unpacking SO much easier. So far (we haven't unpacked everything yet) everything has survived the move, so I'm really impressed with the team for their packing job! I can't recommend Best Moving enough and they were more reasonably priced than some of the major van lines that I looked into.

Poorby Robert E.

I hired Best Moving & Storage to move a smaller load of furniture from Houston, Texas to Sidney, MT. They picked up the furniture on October 23rd. The amount of the move was increased twice. Once because they said geographic location would make it a dead end move and the second time because of a minimum weight requirement. Even with the increases the rate was still reasonable, because I was promised delivery before November 1st. Today is November 19th and they still do not have a delivery date. I spoke with Lisa King (their service representative) and she informed me of a small clause in the contract (very tiny print) that precludes them from shipping date guarantees because of geographic locations. It sounds like geographic locations could mean anywhere in the USA. Lisa wouldn't even give me an approximate delivery date. When I asked if it was weeks or months, she indicated it could be either. I told Lisa I needed the furniture by Thanksgiving and she agreed that renting a truck and coming to their storage facility in Houston and retrieving it may be the only way I would get the furniture in a timely manner. No offer of any type of refund was given. When I asked if I could talk to her (Lisa's) supervisor, she said the supervisor would call me back later that day. Surprise - no phone calls. I hope the someone in the company cares enough about their reputation to resolve this issue and deliver my furniture.
