Not impressed at allby Susan K.

When I made the appointment for our move out of a storage facility to a moving truck, I made sure that they knew we were moving a piano into the moving truck and that we needed a crew that would have experience with that. Also the person I talked to on the phone was Phillip the manager, and he assured me that they would provide shrink wrap to help secure our loose items for the trip and a dolly to move our things into the truck. On the day of the move, the guys arrived near the end of the time window they provided and they were not prepared at all, no dolly, and no shrink wrap. My husband borrowed a dolly from the storage facility and moved most the boxes out of the storage room and moved them to the entrance so it would make it easier for the guys and also since they were later, he wanted to get on the road ASAP. Since we are a military family, we are familiar with moving. My husband said that the guys they sent had no clue on how to pack a truck, which is sad because that is what they are paid to do. Since the guys were inefficient and my husband was tired of telling them where to place the boxes in the truck, he pretty much moved and secured most of the things in the truck. Most important, they didn't bring a piano dolly to move the piano into the truck. Luckily it had wheels, so they just pushed it onto the truck with the help of my husband. The guys from the moving company told my husband, that he was a better mover that most moving guys. We paid for 2 hours of labor but they only did less than hour worth of work because my husband did most of the work for them. Moving is not new to us, so we know when movers are inexperienced, which is sad. The only thing I can say is that we didn't have to pay a lot. Which means we get what we pay for. But it is sad that you have a company that sends out guys that are inexperienced and unprepared for the work. Luckily we will not be back in the El Paso but if we are, we will not use the Sacred Heart. I tried to call Phillip, the manager and left messages, but have not been able to get a hold of him. I call the company and no one picks up the phone, no big surprise. I'm sure other people have had good experiences, I guess it's a luck of the draw for which guys they send to do the job. I hope others have better luck but I just wanted to share so you can get another perspective.
