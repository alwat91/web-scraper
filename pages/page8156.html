3 out of 5
Move Great, Storage, ehby Andria B.

I sold my house in Philly and was moving to the Cherry Hill area in NJ. Between the sale of my house and the purchase of my next house I needed to stay in a short-term apartment. So I wanted my moving company to move me out of my Philly house, bring some of our items to our apartment and then store the rest of our items. Then when it came to moving into my new home, have them get everything from storage, then come and grab the few items at the apartment then finalize the move at the new house.

EverSafe was able to do this. The move out of my house went very smoothly. I can't say enough for the crew (during both times). They're young guys (early 20s) and every single one of them were extremely respectful and I felt good with them (one of the times I was by myself during the move and I didn't have any worries). You pay based on the hourly rate, and these guys didn't abuse that - they worked hard and pushed it the whole time.

The only part where I was not so happy was the storage. We did a quote over the phone and the guys brought the correct sized trucks and the time that they said it would take was accurate. But I got a call later that the storage they originally estimated was only half the amount I needed and the monthly rate doubled. Now, they already had all of my stuff and the fee to get it back (or to get anything I might have needed) would be the cost of one month's rent, so it would have been pointless for me to hire another company to store my things at this point.

Therefore I had no other choice but to pay double the amount I was originally quoted.

The move in to my new place went smooth with another great group of guys doing the move. The only problem was, the 2 beds that were in storage - they lost the screws to put them back together.

Got a call that I should look through all of my boxes because they might be in there - yeah that wasn't going to happen. Finally they jumped in and gave good customer service to correct their problem and one of the guys came out and worked his ass off going back and forth between stores to get the correct bolts and put my beds back together.

So if you're just looking for a move - I highly recommend them.

Storage.... maybe look elsewhere.

EverSafe Moving Co.
