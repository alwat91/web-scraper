Pretty goodby Christine P.

I spoke with Chris initially who gave me an estimate. He was very responsive to my call and my questions, sent over an estimate, reviewed it with me and was very professional. The morning of my move the crew was about 20 minutes late but considering they were coming in to Boston during rush hour I didn't really mind. The crew, 3 guys, worked their butts off. They were all very professional, very nice and friendly. About an hour or so in to the move though it became clear that the truck was not going to be big enough to hold all of our stuff. Things became a bit tense as and I could tell that they were all stressed. The head of the crew said that I underestimated the number of boxes by about 20 which is why truck was too small. We didn't count the boxes but 80-90 boxes for a 2 bedroom seems unlikely. I've moved 12 times in my life, and this is the first time I've ever had this experience. As it was getting later and later, the crew was getting frustrated and so was I. At one point we had to ask the guys to fit as much as they could because they were closing up the truck and there was still space and still a ton of stuff left to be moved. My wife over heard the crew talking (complaining) and one of them asked if we wanted them to take the fridge too. So they loaded up every square inch of the truck (because we wouldn't let them stop until it was done.) when the truck was full there was still a significant amount of small furniture and items left in the apartment.
The unloading process went fairly smoothly.
When they were finished, the head of the crew and I reviewed the estimated and actual charges. It was estimated to take 5.25 hours, it took about 6.5. And there was still a lot of stuff I had to get. I asked to speak with a manager, and spoke with James. I expressed to James that the crew worked hard and the 1.25 additional hours was fine but I was going to have to pay $100 to rent a uhaul and get the rest of the stuff myself. He took $100 off and apologized. Very professional.
Overall the crew worked hard, the managers were nice, but the underestimate of time and truck size was very inconvenient.
I would use them again, but next time I will dramatically overestimate how much stuff I have.
