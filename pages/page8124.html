Before hiring this company I looked up reviews for them & they were good, I don't know who wrote the reviews (friends, relatives??) but were they ever way off. The movers had another job after moving me & he was constantly calling them to find out how much longer it was going to take to finish my move, & since they felt rushed they ended up leaving a couple of my items on the truck. I have tried at least 5 times to get the items back but have not been successful. The communication before the move was excellent. Speedy Men called numerous times to confirm our move and make sure we had everything we needed as well as understand how much there was to move. We paid extra to have the movers early and unfortunately, they were late which messed up our timing at the new home. The good news is that they did not charge us for the early arrival. Overall I would recommend SpeedyMen and would use them on our next move.
