Best Moving company in Nashville we've usedby M V.

We scheduled with Mr. Mover Nashville after an in home estimate for our move from Nashville to Franklin, TN. We received a "not to exceed" amount and felt comfortable knowing that there would be a maximum amount our move would cost. All the other moving companies charged strictly by the hour. The day before our move we received a confirmation call from the office confirming their arrival time and to see if we needed any extra boxes delivered before our move. The next day four movers and 2 trucks arrived at our home, padded our doors, floor and stairway, went over the paperwork and started loading everything up. They wrapped our furniture with moving blankets and were very careful to protect our belongings and our walls. The movers were nice, clean-cut and professional. We were extremely happy with this company and plan on using them again in about 5 years or so.
