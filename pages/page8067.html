4 out of 5
Positive Moving Experienceby Tim B.

Our family was moving from Somerset to Morris county in New Jersey (~25 miles). The customer representative (Dave) was very responsive and offered a fair price for the move of our larger furniture (3 workers, 4 hours). In the 2 days leading up to the move, we received several followup emails/calls confirming the move date and time, including a status update from the mover on his exact time of arrival.

What was concerning us was when the movers arrived, they went over the cost estimate given to them by the company representative, which was for 6 hours of work and considerably more money (this seemed a bit dishonest on the part of the customer rep). Luckily, after some debating and showing the signed quote, the workers were willing to adhere to the originally agreed upon amount.

The workers themselves were very professional and took good care of our items during the move (everything was thoroughly wrapped and padded.) The move took roughly 4.5 hours total (basically as planned), and there were no items damaged in the process.

Advice: be very specific about what you want moved (although the movers will allow for minor amendments if necessary). Make sure to have the agreed upon quote on hand during move day.
