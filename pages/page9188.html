Dishonest, unethical, unprofessional company. by Alexandra G.

My entire experience with International Van Lines was shocking and unbelievable and its description is six pages long.

Here are just very, very few details of my experience:
Due to medical emergency, I needed to move fast and was moving out of a very small apartment. I called International Van Lines and they gave me a quote over the phone of $3700 and I paid a deposit of $1400. Little did I know that the quote meant absolutely nothing.

As soon as the moving crew arrived, they demanded $5000 in cash, in addition to the $1400 deposit, before they even saw the items I needed to move, claiming that I had way more than expected.

When everything was packed into the moving truck I was told that I had to pay $6000 in cash. (Again, I was moving out of a very small apartment.)

When the moving truck arrived at my house three hours late, the movers immediately demanded almost $300 more, in addition to $6000 that I already paid, for what they called "long carry" and stairs in the house. According to the contract, movers can charge $75 if a driveway is longer than 75 feet. They charged me $150 without any reason or explanation stating that if they don't get the money right away, in cash, they will leave all my things on the driveway.

When I told the movers that I did not have that much cash and needed to go to the bank they began to threaten me that they'll charge me more "for the wait."

Before the movers entered my house, I asked them specifically to protect the stairs which they refused to do. They damaged the stairs. And, of course, there were other damages as well.

They did not finish the job at my house. They told me that they had another job and simply walked out.

All my furniture, clothes, even dishes were soaked with cigarette smoke through and through.

I tried to tell the company about the disgusting behavior of the movers and about my horrible experience. They did not care one bit and took no responsibility at all neither for the damages nor for the extra charges.

My experience with International Van Lines can only be described as robbery and abuse. I will never recommend this company to anyone.

Horrifying Damage, Disrespectby Michael H.

I’m not quite sure where to start with this review. The short version is as follows. My wife and I paid IVL over $7,000 to move us from New Jersey to Indiana. We were told that the movers would wait to deliver our items to the destination until we arrived a few days later. The next day, I received a call telling me that they were going to deliver the items the following day, with or without us.
Since we were not present for the delivery, the movers were downright careless with our items. Many hard plastic boxes were broken, and virtually all of the cardboard boxes ended up in terrible shape. Several pieces of furniture were also destroyed. See the pictures for clarification.
Furthermore, the movers ignored our request to spread the items across the storage unit so that they would be easier to access. We had arranged for a unit that was roughly twice the size that we needed in order to make this task easier. In spite of this, the movers stacked our items haphazardly on top of each other, with furniture piled on top of fragile items, all the while leaving half of the floor space unused. We had to hire local movers just to rearrange our items in such a way that we could access them. Those movers said that the way that the IVL movers had arranged our items was “scary” and felt that their behavior was downright dangerous.
When I voiced my displeasure to the company, I was initially ignored. Once I was finally spoken to, the manager attempted to intimidate me into accepting an appallingly small sum of “hush money.” He assured me that their lawyers would bully us into either accepting a pitifully small settlement (and signing an agreement preventing us from telling anyone about our horrifying experience) or take them to court, which he assured me would be stacked against us.
My conscience prevented me from taking the deal. I felt obligated to let others know about our horrendous experience, in the hope that future customers will be able to avoid this misery. This is not a company that can be trusted to handle your belongings, and it is not a company with enough integrity to fix their mistakes. Do not, under any circumstances, entrust your possessions to them.
