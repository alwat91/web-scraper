Missing stuff-- bad attitude and a run aroundby Josh p.

We used Al’s to move in March 2017. We were only moving about 5 miles away—but had to get stuff out of a full storage unit as well up in Yorktown to transport to the new house.

Price wise they came in way cheaper than everyone else we spoke to.

The move itself went smooth and we tried our best to take care of the guys actually moving as it had just snowed and was cold and icy out.

After the move we started going through all the items and unpacking—only to find some items missing we couldn’t account for. We called al’s and they asked us to go through everything and confirm and then call back.

So that’s what we did. We found one box that had been placed inside a kitchen island and then taped closed. However we were still missing 2 un-opened wedding presents and a box of clothes.

It was snowy out and a bit of a complicated move so anything’s possible and didn’t want to accuse anyone of stealing—but the items did not make it and were left behind somewhere.

When we called Al’s we were given a run around of “someone will call you back” when we finally spoke to someone we were told “we’ve been in business forever our guys don’t lose or steal things” and then “you can submit an insurance claim but it will probably be denied”

Overall how they handled this situation was horrible and while they are cheaper I’d say STAY AWAY from Al’s. If something goes wrong they will NOT stand behind their work.

We are missing several hundred dollars in wedding presents and a box of clothes. We have been through the entire house, opened every box, looking in every crevice and its not here.

We don’t know if it was negligence or theft—but either way I’d stay away and not trust them. Very untrustworthy.
