Millennium Movers

( 3.9 overall, based on 27 reviews)

303-650-2891303-650-2891

Denver, CO 80216

We provide a range of services and materials needed to relocate you to your new home, storage, or office. Our services range from residential & commercial moving services, packing services, and short or long term secure storage. We have a full selection of boxes and packing materials as well. We specialize in local residential moving in the Denver metro and Boulder areas. We also move clients regionally within a 400 mile radius from Denver, which includes moves going within the states that are adjacent to Colorado. Our local moving services are normally charged on an hourly rate basis. If an estimate is needed, usually 3 bedroom + or commercial locations, we can send a representative to your home. For loading or unloading customer’s truck, container, storage unit, or you just need movers to move items internally we can send you a crew of movers without a truck. This will result in a smaller trip charge because our truck was not needed. For storage needs we provide safe and secure storage. Your items would be placed in secure vaults for mid to long term storage. The storage vaults or containers are around 8’ X 5’ X 8’ in dimensions. The monthly storage cost starts at $49/mo. For very short term storage around a week or two; we normally section off a part of an area of our warehouse for this type of shipment. We make sure each shipment is separated from other shipments. When is comes to Packing services, we can provide one or as many packers needed. This can be done on an hourly rate basis or charged per each box packed. The labor charge for us to pack by the box, range from a small box being the least costly, to dish packs being the most costly box to pack. We also have a full selection of moving boxes and packing materials. So when it comes to picking a mover to move you, choose Millennium Movers. You'll find that we are very affordable and professional.
