Specialties:
Two Men and a Truck® offers you a full line of home moving and home packing services in and around the Chattanooga area. Our goal is to exceed your expectations by customizing our moving services to your specific needs. So, whether you're moving across Chattanooga, across Tennessee, or even across the country, we're the perfect Chattanooga movers to fit your needs. We move apartments, condominiums and homes - no job is too large or too small.

History:
Established in 1999.

Two Men and a Truck® started in the early 1980s as a way for two brothers to make extra money while in high school. Nearly 20 years later, the company has grown to more than 200 locations worldwide. Today, Two Men and a Truck® is the nation's largest franchised local moving company, and it boasts a fleet of more than 1,400 trucks. The company has come a long way. The Two Men and a Truck® system continues to pave the way for future growth and innovation in home and business moving, while maintaining a commitment to exceeding customers' expectations.

Meet the Business Owner:
Two Men and a Truck® Chattanooga was started in 1999. Joey and Jennifer Hale are the franchisees for the locations and have received numerous awards for the franchise's success in the Chattanooga area. The Chattanooga location recently received recognition for its achievement if areas such as high standards in customer service, community involvement, and operational excellence. The Chattanooga office was ranked 2nd out of more than 200 franchises.

We used Two Men and a Truck Chattanooga to move a few months ago and
had a wonderful experience! They quoted us up to 7 hours for our move
and it barely took 5. That was with every piece of our brand new
furniture being meticulously wrapped, padded, and carefully
loaded/unloaded on/off the truck as well. I was very involved with the
move and can honestly say that the movers David, Tim, and Terrin seemed
to care more about the safety and condition of my belongings than I did!

Our apartment was up 3 flights of stairs and we were moving to another
2nd floor apartment and they sent a 3rd mover free of charge to make
sure everything went quickly and smoothly! That was an unexpected and a
very welcome surprise that surely saved us quite a bit of money. Their
motto "movers who care" definitely holds true for this location. They
arrived several minutes early and got to work immediately. At no point
did anyone take a break or waste time, I even suggested that they could
stop for a few moments to catch their breath, but they said they did
this all the time and were conditioned for it. We were impressed by
their work ethic to say the least and I would highly recommend you
request the movers they sent us. Make sure you give them a healthy tip
if you do, they'll definitely earn it!

Scheduling was very easy and only required a small deposit to reserve
our date and was credited to the bill at the end of the move. We had a
coupon for their service, but misplaced it while packing and sent it to
them weeks after the move and were promptly mailed a check for the value
of the coupon.

Their customer service is excellent all the way around, but especially
the movers. I did not expect the level of attention they paid to every
little detail and feel that we got an excellent value for our money.
While their prices may be a little higher than average you most
definitely get what you pay for with moving companies and the Two Men
and a Truck Chattanooga location is worth every penny. If you're looking
for a quality moving service that values your time, cares for your
possessions every step of the way, and acts with a sense of urgency in
everything they do then this is the moving company for you
