I chose Big League Movers because they were by far the best customer service experience of the 3 quotes I received for my move! They got in touch with me quickly and I spoke with Kevin. I was on a tight budget and I really appreciated them doing that. On the day of my move, they gave me a window of 1:00-3:00. At about noon, they did call and say the movers were running late. I can't say enough that this was a great experience with Big League Movers! Each phone call I had, they answered my questions fully and were kind and patient.
