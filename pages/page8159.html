5 out of 5
Superb Service & Price!by Cynthia J.

I initially found My Mountain Movers (MMM) on AngiesList.com and then did a Google search as well to view their website. About 3 weeks before the move, I contacted MMM and spoke with the owner, Tony. Based upon the other quotes I had received, he was $10 per hour cheaper than his competitors. The only thing I didn't quite like which I found to be common in the KC metro area is that movers in KC Metro charge a one-time flat truck/transportation fee generally the same rate as the per hour fee. But since the other companies I contacted had the same type of one-type fee, there's not much I could do with it except go with it instead of causing a fuss. As many times as I have moved, I know almost exactly how many boxes and what I need since I'm quite the minimalist in regards to furniture and "stuff".

Side-note: This was my 19th move in 15 years!! No, I'm not joking. I've moved myself, have had family helped and have hired numerous professional movers for local moves.

I was moving from a 1st floor apartment in OP to a walk-out basement in a home/residential neighborhood 15 miles east in SKC. It turned out that the new roommate that was taking over my lease needed to move sooner than my original move date. This happened very quickly since I was out of town at the time. I called Tony 3 days before the date I needed to change it too. He called me back within 10 minutes and said they could change it from April 2 to March 25th. I told him I would have the majority of my things packed like we had originally discussed 3 weeks ago, and I confirmed the guys would call me before arriving at my apartment.

The night before the move, I got sick from side effects from some medication I'm taking from a car wreck. Since I'm single with no friends or family nearby, I had no help to help me pack my things prior to the movers arriving since I was out of town prior to the moving date. I called Tony, unfortunately, 4-5 hours before the scheduled move the day-of and notified and apologized to him that I would be unable to pack my things like we originally discussed. He called me back and left me a voicemail that it would be NO PROBLEM, add'l boxes would be packed, and it would not be an issue for the guys to pack my items and restated the hourly rate. I understood that morning, my original quote would go up but at the time based upon how I was feeling, I really had no other choice. I packed what I could and left it at that.

The employees, Scott & Quinton, called me 20 minutes before arriving and gave me their 'estimated' arrival time based upon traffic. They arrived EXACTLY at the time they gave me over the phone. They had uniforms on (Khaki pants and polo shirts), no sagging pants, were extremely professional, spoke fluent English (no communication gap - woohoo!) were polite and friendly to me the entire time. I gave them the initial walk-through and then they began to work. They were great at asking me questions if they were unsure instead of just tossing random things in random boxes. Despite their age (early 20s), they were the most politest moving men, I have dealt with in over 5 years! They packed my huge TVs and computer items VERY well and cleaned up their mess at the end. When we arrived at the new place, we did another walk-through and they quickly got to work unloading and setting up. Since I'm unable to lift items over 10 lbs, they did NOT get an attitude or were rude when I asked them to move an item 2-3 times so I could get it in the best spot for its function. Quinton & Scott asked me several times as they were wrapping up to ensure I didn't need anything else rearranged since they understood my circumstances from the wreck I'm recovering from. Scott was very detailed and answered any questions I had when going over the receipt with me.

The final amount came in at $135 above budget BUT this was due to my own fault of being sick and not being able to pack myself before they arrived like what was originally discussed & planned in addition to the extra boxes being used as well. I am SO GRATEFUL Tony, Quinton & Scott were able to work with me on my last minute changes (date change, packing changes, more boxes needed, additional time to pack, etc) related to my move.

I highly recommend My Mountain Movers to ANYONE especially single people who don't have help from friends or live near family. You will feel safe and comfortable with My Mountain Movers. They are a very professional yet personable crew. They are the BEST IN KC METRO!
