WE ARE WORKING WITH BINDING ESTIMATES OR FLAT PRICES ONLY !!!

In order to provide an honest Flat Price quote, we have to schedule a FREE IN HOME estimate before the move.

An in-home moving estimate is our most accurate indicator of what you can expect to spend on your move. We will complete a detailed inventory list of everything that needs to be moved and we'll be able to determine whether you need any additional services , like packing, help with specialized items or in-transit storage. During your estimate, we will note items that need special care (i.e. flat screens, plasmas, china hutches, items with glass or marble tops, shelves, mirrors etc).

An in-home appointment is designed to help you appropriately plan for moving day or days. There are a number of benefits to scheduling an estimate appointment .
Review the list below :
•Face to face meeting with your movers.
•An opportunity to explain which items are going and which are not.
•Receive one on one answers to questions.
•Create a realistic picture of your moving expenses.
•Written moving quote with a listing of services.
Please let us know your availability and we will schedule a Free in-home estimate for you.
