No bad, but could be betterby Michael H.

This was not an unpleasant experience but could have been better. I had to give information on the move over the phone AND have them visit. My move was very simple and straightforward yet the estimate I received on the phone was not the same as what then was quoted to me after the visit which was of course accompanied by the request for a $100 deposit. I accommodated their request to move a day earlier and did not get a benefit for this. On the day of the move, they showed up 15 minutes late with a 3-man crew and got right to work. They use uniformed full-time employees they know and not rent-a-labor. The movers were pleasant and hard-worker which is essential given that they charge by the hour -- all the risk is on the customer. I chose to leave all beds/furniture assembled so that they would have the ability to re-assemble completely. The crew did a good job of not damaging the old apartment and worked consistently throughout the move of my 3-bedroom apartment. Once at the new home, they thought they were done without fully reassembling everything, Even after they left, the top bunk bed was put together incorrectly, having left it unsafe with the top bunk guardrail not properly attached.. A 2nd visit to fix the problem could not -- as they lost a part of the bed. They could have done a much better job of ensuring customer satisfaction but they were in such a rush to leave having overstepped their estimate. I found the moving crew to be pleasant, hard-working and professional but just not enough attention to detail and overall job satisfaction to get the highest mark. I paid about $1250 all-in to move the 3-bedroom apartment (with not too much furniture) into my 4-bedroom house which was the estimated price.
